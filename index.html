1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to iShare Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our file transfer application.

2. HOW iShare PRO WORKS

iShare Pro is a peer-to-peer (P2P) file sharing tool.

  • Files are transferred directly between devices using your local Wi-Fi network or Hotspot.
  • We DO NOT upload your files to any external server or cloud storage.
  • We DO NOT view, access, or store the content of the files you share.

3. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function:

  • Device Name: Used to identify your device to other users nearby. This is stored locally on your device.
  • Network Information: Used temporarily to discover other devices on the same network.

4. PERMISSIONS WE REQUEST

To function correctly, iShare Pro requires the following permissions:

  • Storage / Photos & Videos: To select files to send and to save received files.
  • Location / Nearby Devices: Required by the Android system to discover local Wi-Fi networks and nearby devices. We do not track your physical GPS location.
  • Wi-Fi/Network State: To manage connections between devices.

5. THIRD-PARTY SHARING

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties. Since files are transferred locally, no third party has access to your transfers.
